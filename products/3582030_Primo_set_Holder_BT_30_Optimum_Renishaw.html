Primo™ Radio Part Setter (workpiece measuring probe)
  • This probe automatically determines the workpiece datum before machining and performs in-process measurements of the rough or finish machined workpiece dimensions.
Primo™ Radio 3D Tool Setter (workpiece measuring probe)
  • This probe is used to automatically measure the length and diameter of a tool.
Primo™ Interface (Interface)
  • The interface communicates with the workpiece and tool probe and the machine tool control via Renishaw's highly reliable FHSS (Frequency Hopping Spread Spectrum) radio transmission.
Primo Credit Token (credit token)
  • Each Primo kit includes the first 6-month credit token. The 6-month token allows the Primo system to operate for six months. It offers flexibility in terms of credit payment, as during quieter periods you can delay the purchase of a Primo credit token until you need your Primo system again. The upgrade credit token - Primo CREDIT-U - offers indefinite use of the Primo system with a one-time payment.
GoProbe Software
  • Supports cycles for workpiece and tool measurement and calibration. All cycles use a single line of code, making the software quick to learn and easy to use in daily operations.
Includes:
  • Manual, programming disk, app, e-training, training workpiece, programming manual.
Primo Total Protect (PTP) (Primo Total Protection)
  • Primo Complete Protection is an extended warranty to protect your investment if you have activated a credit token. If your Primo probe is accidentally damaged during this period, your Renishaw office will supply you with a free replacement. Renishaw's terms and conditions apply.
