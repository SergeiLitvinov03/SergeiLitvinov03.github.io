Energy savings through frequency-controlled compressors

Reducing energy consumption and protecting our precious energy resources has become a global challenge given its impact on the environment. The A-PLUS range, with outputs of 22, 37, 55 and 75 kW, is also available in a speed control version that combines high performance with energy saving solutions. By using a variable speed compressor in a typical installation, you save a significant amount of energy.

A frequency converter dynamically adjusts the voltage, frequency and current values ​​of the motor. The air output is constantly set to 50 to 100 percent of the full load of the compressor. This gives the following measurable positive effects:
  • Accurate coverage of the air requirement by continuous control of the engine speed and compressed air generation
  • Minimization of unnecessary energy losses by continuous adjustment of the compressed air generation to the actual air requirement
  • Energy consumption proportional to the supplied compressed air
  • Constant and precise pressure control, adjustable to any value between 6 and 10 bar (13 bar on request)
