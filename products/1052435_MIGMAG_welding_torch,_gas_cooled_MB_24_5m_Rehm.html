Handy MIG / MAG torch for universal use in metal construction and general locksmith work. Up / Down version for controlling the welding energy before the start of welding and during welding. The GRIP handle shell with ball joint guarantees a high grip and optimal handling. Thanks to soft components in the grip area and thumb rest, the torch remains reliably in the hand, even in extreme situations. Manufacturer Rehm GmbH & Co.
