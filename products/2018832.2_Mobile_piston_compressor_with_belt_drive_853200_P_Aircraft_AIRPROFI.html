Quality electric motor

  • Quality electric motor with high torque is equipped with a motor protection switch
  • Start-up capacitor and start-up relief valve ensure easy start-up
  • As standard with phase inverter for easy manual reversal of the direction of rotation

Compressed air compression via high-performance aggregate HOS

  • The start-up relief valve ensures a light and pressure-free start
  • High-performance unit made of gray cast iron and the low speed guarantee particularly quiet, low-vibration operation and a long service life
  • This ensures an even longer life due to even less wear
  • High-quality compressor with optimum lubrication for a long service life and high reliability
  • Equipped with high quality suction filter
  • The aftercooler with large cooling fins ensures a low boiler inlet temperature and thus less moisture in the compressed air, which protects the compressed air tools
  • In addition with intercooler to the aftercooler
  • Corrosion-free valve plates for improved heat dissipation and longevity of the compressor
  • The large fan wheel and a cooling air baffle on the cylinder ensure optimum cooling of the compressor
  • A sturdy belt guard made of metal ensures optimum protection of the components and high user safety
  • Compressed air supply into the boiler via flexible armored hose line with heat protection prevents vibration fracture

High quality drive belt

  • Powerful and economical drive
  • The toothed drive belt facilitates start-up, improves smoothness and power transmission and reduces the Power consumption up to 30%
  • Stable belt guard made of metal

Operation at the maintenance block

  • Equipped with a high-quality CONDOR pressure switch MDR 5 with robust push button function for fully automatic on-off operation
  • Filter pressure regulator for stepless adjustment of the working pressure, with simultaneous dirt, oil and condensation separation
  • Two large pressure gauges indicate the tank and working pressure
  • Air extraction at the maintenance block via a comfort safety quick coupling
  • Only fixed screw connections at the maintenance block, no hose connections

Two compressed air supply points

  • A compressed air extraction point for filtered compressed air
  • A compressed air extraction point via direct outlet on the boiler with ball valve

Comfort safety clutch - the advantages.

  • Comfort safety couplings from AIRCRAFT are the first manufacturer to be used as standard
  • Two separate locking systems for maximum work safety
  • Dangerous flogging of the hose is prevented

Comfortable and safe to transport and set up

  • Ergonomically extended handle for easy transport in a nearly upright position - stress on the back by lifting is avoided
  • Puncture-proof PU foam wheels and stable steering gear with parking brake enable easy transport
  • The parking brake prevents rotation and rolling of the wheels and ensures an optimal level of the compressor
  • With practical second transport handle on the boiler, with which the carrying of the compressor is facilitated

High-quality boiler with durable rust protection

  • AIRCRAFT is the first manufacturer to equip the compressors of the AIRPROFI series as standard with internally and externally hot-dip galvanized boilers
  • The AIRPROFI series boilers therefore come with a 15-year rust-proof warranty.
