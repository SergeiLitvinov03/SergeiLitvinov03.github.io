  • Two-column guided bandsaw for sawing solid bars and bar profiles
  • Mitsubishi FX 5 control with easy operation via 7" touch screen
  • 700mm material feed as standard
  • Cutting movement via hydraulic cylinder
  • With BRP (cutting feed control) -> when a set ampere limit is exceeded, the feed movement is stopped while the saw band continues to run in order to convey chips out of the cutting channel. After reaching the normal load, the sawing process is continued
  • Robust machine body and smallest possible distances between saw band, machine guide and workpiece minimize vibrations for highest sawing performance
  • Infinitely variable cutting speed from 15 - 150 m/min
  • Saw band is driven by bevel gear, asynchronous motor and frequency converter
  • Clamping of the material in front of and behind the cut by two-part vice
  • Pressure adjustment of the hydraulic vice tension via control valve with manometer
  • saw blade inclination of 7Â° and hydraulic saw blade tensioning increase the service life of the saw blade
  • front band guide moves automatically parallel to the movable vice jaw
  • electrically driven chip clearing brush
  • emulsion cooling with liquid distribution into the saw band guides and directly into the cutting channel
  • With automatic material feed: frequency-controlled feed via circulating spindle; optical sensor detects when no more material is loaded and pauses the material feed
  • Standard with chip conveyor and chip container
  • More powerful motor optionally available
