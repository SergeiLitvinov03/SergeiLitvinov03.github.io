With practical shelves for all individual segments and bending rails


Extremely fast and easy operation - in just a few steps you can turn your sheets into the desired shape without great effort


Step 1:


  • Place the workpiece on the magnetic conductors and adjust using the bending edge

Step 2:


  • Choose the right bending segment, place it on the workpiece and activate the magnet

Step 3:


  • At the bend stop you can set the exact bending angle. Perform the bending process

The conversion for further bending operations is done in a few steps.



Note: Do not use the machine with pacemakers
