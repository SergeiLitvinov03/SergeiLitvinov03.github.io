pc(s).
  • For fully automatic removal of condensate or other non-aggressive fluids from the compressed air system
  • A capacitive sensor detects the level of the condensate. This is very accurate and requires no mechanical floats and contacts.
  • For installation as external arresters on reciprocating or screw compressors, aftercoolers, cyclone separators, compressed air boilers, compressed air dryers and compressed air filters
  • Equipped as standard with a factory alarm, LED indicator, test button and integrated sieve
  • On request also version with service network for setting the diagnostic parameters and an alarm output available
function Description
  • The condensate is collected in the collection reservoir.
  • If the level is high enough, the condensate is discharged from the system without loss of air
  • Liquid level is detected by a precise capacitive liquid level sensor
  • The special self-cleaning, direct-acting valve ensures reliable operation

  • Integrated sieve (easy access / cleaning)
