  • Boiler batteries for up to 400 liters of additional air supply
  • Available as 2-pack and 4-pack batteries
  • All tank batteries also available in 13 bar version with 75 l tanks
  • By connecting additional compressed air tanks, the tank volume is increased, whereby a higher air supply can be built up.
  • Increased air demand can be covered at short notice
  • If required, individual compressed air tanks can be disconnected from the compressed air supply via ball valves, which reduces the tank volume accordingly and thus builds up the pressure more quickly.
  • Fifteen-year warranty against rusting through on the inside and outside of hot-dip galvanized air reservoirs.
  • All tank batteries can be easily moved with the lift truck
