Replaces the control MEGA 120. Allows the complete control of the machine by the PLC function and the encoder positioned on the pinion of the feed chain

  • 7 "- 16: 9 Wide Touch Screen Color Screen
  • Graphic representation of all machining units and their condition (ON / OFF)
  • Graphical simulation of the operations to be carried out for each side of the board
  • 60 work programs with configuration directly from the operator
  • Control of the processing units and automatic activation of the motors
  • Adjustment and regulation of the temperature of the glue pot
  • Choice for longitudinal and transverse processing without program change
  • Simple and guided diagnosis for the simple correction of possible errors
  • Statistics on absolute data and individual processing: number of pieces, edge consumption, operating hours of the machine, total chain feed chain
  • Ambient temperature range: from 0 ° to + 45 ° C
  • Unit of measurement selectable in mm or inches
