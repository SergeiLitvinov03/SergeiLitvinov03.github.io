Automatic switch-on
  • When the double grinder is switched on, the extraction system starts automatically with a delay of 2-3 seconds. There is no need to additionally switch on the suction unit
  • The extraction system continues to run for 2-3 seconds after the double grinder is switched off
  • The residual dust is extracted after the machine is switched off in accordance with the Hazardous Substances Ordinance
