The Grippex bar gripper increases production and automation on your CNC lathe! Only a limited percentage of all CNC lathes currently installed are equipped with bar feeders. There are various reasons for this, such as the relatively high procurement costs or lack of space in the workshop. For small batch production, however, it is often sufficient to use a bar feeder that makes use of the conditions already present on the machine, such as a hollow spindle and hollow clamping cylinder. These facilities only need to be supplemented with a guide tube and the GRIPPEX bar gripper. The Grippex bar gripper can be accommodated in almost any lathe turret. Advantages and main features of the GRIPPEX bargripper
  • Quick and easy assembly - The working range of the gripper can be fully used without retooling
  • Light and compact - Therefore full use of the turret possible
  • Withstands high coolant pressure (20 bar). Therefore a pressure drop valve is not necessary
  • Operates reliably from 0.5 bar coolant pressure
  • Grips directly on the chuck - results in stable parting-off of the turned parts.
  • Grips with 3 legs - therefore problem-free machining of hexagonal bars in any angular position of the spindle
  • Equipped with robotic gripper jaws, the device can also be used as a workpiece gripper.
