Automatic switch-on for extraction systems
  • As soon as a machine is switched on, EasySwitch switches on your suction device. After the last machine has been switched off, the suction system runs for approx. 10 seconds to remove the remaining chips from the pipe system.
  • Easy installation, no intervention in the building installation necessary
  • The extraction only works when needed (energy saving).
  • Machinery and workshop remain dust-free.
  • Preventive health protection
  • Easy to dismantle, just take with you when moving
  • For all extraction systems up to 4 kW, without undervoltage release
