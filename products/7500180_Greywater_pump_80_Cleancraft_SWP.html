Versatile and powerful
  • Pumping of ponds, pumping out of basements eg in case of flooding, irrigation of beds
  • Sucks off even with sand, cleaning agent or oil polluted water, eg sewage, sludge, ...
  • Approximately 2.5 hours running time with one tank filling at 75% load

Robust, durable and reliable

  • Functional in any weather
  • Spiral housing and impeller made of cast iron, thus highest robustness and long life of the pump, even when used with sand-containing water
  • Powerful and efficient 4-stroke engine, easy to start in all conditions
  • Robust steel frame with vibration dampers
  • Built-in membrane prevents the reflux of water

Easy to handle

  • Quick and easy commissioning by direct generation of the negative pressure and connection of the hoses to the pump
  • Easy maintenance
