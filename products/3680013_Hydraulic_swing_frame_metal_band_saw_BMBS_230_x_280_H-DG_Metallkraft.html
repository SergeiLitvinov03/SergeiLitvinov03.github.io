  • For sawing hollow profiles and solid materials made of steel, stainless steel, tool steel, non-ferrous metals and plastic
  • Mitre adjustment from the front for better handling with attached roller conveyor
  • Robust band guides with adjustable carbide plates for a precise cut
  • Saw blade guides with cooling system
  • Electromechanical monitoring of the saw blade tension via microswitch
  • With chip clearing brush for optimum cleaning and maintenance of the saw blade
  • When the saw arm is raised, the lowering cylinder locks automatically in the upper position - no additional locking on the cylinder is required
  • Saw arm tilted by 25°, thus extending the service life of the saw blade
  • The saw blade stops automatically at the end of the cutting process
  • Lowering of the saw arm by gravity or manually (H-DG)
  • Turntable with swiveling cutting gap: No cutting into the saw table, therefore always a flat contact surface and therefore greater cutting accuracy
  • Ergonomic operating concept
  • Clamping lever for angle adjustment from the front: faster adjustment of the cutting angle
  • Mounting adapter for roller conveyors included in the scope of delivery
  • Vice with quick clamping device
