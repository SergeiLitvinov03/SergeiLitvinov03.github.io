  • Thanks to patented technology, regular service is possible in only 30 seconds and without expensive cleaning
  • Wall and floor mounting possible
  • Works reliably with any steam trap
  • Suitable for all common compressor oils
  • Operating media: condensate (air, water, oil), non-aggressive, not suitable for emulsion
  • Quick and clean change of the filter cartridge
  • No settling tank necessary (no bacteria formation)
  • Easy installation due to compact design and small dimensions
  • Connection via quick couplings
Function Description:
  • Multi-stage oil-water separation via an oil-attracting filter and activated carbon
  • After the end of the service life of the filter element this is unscrewed and disposed of completely
  • Filter element is tightly closed with plastic cover, so that residual condensate can remain in it
  • Locked filter element can be disposed of according to current regulations
service interval
  • after occurrence of one of the following three cases:
  • 4,000 operating hours of the compressor at compressor oil transfer of 2.5 mg / m3
  • Discoloration of the polypropylene medium - analysis by simple visual inspection
  • At the latest after 12 months (regardless of the operating hours of the compressor)
