SYMplus Milling for cost-effective, time-reduced and economical work. SYMplus Milling is the ideal software supplement for all Siemens controlled OPTIMUM milling machines.
As a training software, SYMplus Milling supports fast familiarization with DIN programming according to PAL and SIEMENS. Above all, SYMplus is a particularly easy-to-learn CAD / CAM system that saves you programming time, avoids crashes, reduces production time and, with a uniform interface, NC programs for different OPTIMUM machines or SIEMENS controllers (802C, 808D , 840D, ...).
CAD geometry creation Unmatched fast and easy, you can graphically program workpieces with SYMplus, even if the drawing is not dimensioned to meet NC requirements. Alternatively, you can use CAD contours from DXF.
CAM - Workplan Creation
The processing is also graphically defined by means of pictograms. It is very easy to compare production strategies and thus optimize processing. The time calculation helps with the calculation. In this case, the residual material detection refers to the entire process, the blank is tracked continuously.
The 2D simulation shows many details such as: B. oversizes and the cutting track of each individual milling path. Here you can also "take" control measures. The 3D simulation gives the best overview of the machining. The actual NC program can be generated with just a few clicks and transmitted to the controller, eg via USB stick.
SYMplus milling runs under Windows 7 and Windows 8. Further information and additional modules on request.
