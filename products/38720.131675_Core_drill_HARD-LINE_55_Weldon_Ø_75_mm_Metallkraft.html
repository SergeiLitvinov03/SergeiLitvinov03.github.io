Properties of the carbide-tipped HARD-LINE core drills:
Pre-Intermediate-trimmer geometry
  • manufactured in elaborate pre-middle trimmer geometry
  • Thus: vibration-free, quiet and easy cutting with maximum service life

Conical spiral
  • Made with conical spiral
  • Thus: clean chip flow and highest cutting performance, even with difficult materials (heavy-duty machinists)

Sandvik carbide teeth
  • Only equipped with Sandvik carbide teeth
  • NOTE: Only the best carbide is good enough for Karnasch core drills
