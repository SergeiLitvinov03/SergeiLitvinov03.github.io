The Pro V 65E / Pro V 65LE bar feeder is the ideal solution for automatic loading of CNC lathes with short bars. It combines maximum productivity with a small footprint and is equipped with a magazine in the E variant for up to 1,200 mm bar length and in the LE variant for up to 1,500 mm. Simple operating unit and remote control A user-friendly operating unit and the remote control ensure the interaction between the loading magazine and the lathe. This allows the production process to be carried out safely and efficiently.
