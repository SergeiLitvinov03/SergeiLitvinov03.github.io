  • Micro-metering unit without housing, easy to install in just a few steps
  • Minimal quantities of air/oil particles are delivered directly to the contact point between tool and material, reducing friction and significantly improving machining processes.
  • The tiny quantities of lubricant are delivered with extreme precision through a capillary tube to the lubrication point with the aid of micro-volumetric pumps.
  • This leads to an immediate increase in machine productivity, an increase in tool life and the elimination of the time required to dispose of the lubricant, the consumption of which is significantly reduced.
  • Advantages: Increased tool life, increased productivity, clean working environment, reduced friction, reduced lubricant waste
