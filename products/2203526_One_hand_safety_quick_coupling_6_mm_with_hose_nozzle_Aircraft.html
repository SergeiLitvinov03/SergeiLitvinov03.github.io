Your benefits at a glance:

work safety

  • Parts and dirt particles are not thrown away
  • No whipping of the hose as in a conventional quick disconnect coupling
  • The system fulfills the requirements of ISO 4414 and is BIA compliant. Increased safety standard in the workplace as standard!
  • Very high flow rate
  • Absolute tightness of couplings and threads
Economic benefits and stability
  • Long life of the coupling due to high-quality workmanship and reduced mechanical stress
  • Low energy costs due to the long lasting tightness of the coupling
  • Plug nipple is not exposed to mechanical stress, brass nipples also last longer
  • Resistant to abrasion, impact, bruising, vibration and corrosion
  • Lightweight, antistatic and scratch-resistant material
Comfort
  • Safety couplings - incomparably easy to use
  • With a push of a button, the pressure is reduced, with another push of a button compressed air systems and tools are safely separated
  • Pressure-free domes
  • Ergonomic, compact form for intuitive and natural handling
  • By rotating housing, the position of the control knob is variably adjustable
  • Reduced dimensions for low space consumption, easy installation
