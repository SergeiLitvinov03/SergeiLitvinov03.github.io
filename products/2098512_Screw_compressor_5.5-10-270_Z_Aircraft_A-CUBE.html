Coaxial screw-type compressor with maintenance-free drive and maximum energy efficiency in all pressure ranges. Low compressor speeds and low load on the bearings of the patented direct drive ensure a very long service life.

EasyTRONIC Micro II electronic control for reliability and efficiency
  • Prevents motor overload, reduces wear and makes for a longer service life
  • Monitoring of operating hours, temperature and motor overload
  • Phase sequence relays control the direction of rotation of the motor, thereby avoiding possible damage during commissioning or after relocation
Optimized cooling system
  • Low-noise centrifugal fan guarantees optimal operating temperatures even under unfavourable conditions
  • Thermostatically and electronically controlled fan and generously sized heat exchanger result in a longer service life and less fluctuation in oil temperature
User-friendly in all details
  • Low noise, suitable for deployment in any work environment
  • Small footprint facilitates transport and deployment
  • Service-friendly thanks to optimum accessibility and easy removal and installation of all components that require maintenance
  • All air and oil lines are made of heat-resistant rubber
  • Equipped with a star delta circuitry as factory standard
  • Ready to connect with all fittings and safety equipment
K models with Integrated refrigeration dryer
  • More reliable compressed air system without costly downtimes and production delays while simultaneously ensuring product quality
  • Fully integrated into the housing
  • Ready for use without installation overhead
  • Equipped with control panel and time-controlled condensate drain
