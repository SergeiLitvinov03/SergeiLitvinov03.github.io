Effective filtration of dirt particles in three levels:


  1. Level: Absorption of coarse dirt particles in the filter bag

  2. Level: Dust particles are bound in water

  3. Level: Finest particles are separated in the cartridge filter

  • Efficient operation due to stepless power regulation via rotary knob
  • Blower function for cleaning hard-to-reach places
  • Manual filter cleaning of the cartridge filter for long-lasting filter effect
